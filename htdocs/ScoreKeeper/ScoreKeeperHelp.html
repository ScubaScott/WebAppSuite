Score Keeper Help

Instructions for using the timer, scoring, and history features.

Back

Timer Controls

  • Remaining: shows the current time left in the selected period. Tap it while the timer is stopped to open the time-limit selector.
  • Elapsed: shows the elapsed time within the current period. Tap it while running to adjust elapsed time if the clock was started early or late.

Time Limit Options

  • Choose one of the preset limits: 12 min, 20 min, 35 min, or 40 min.
  • Tap Manual to enter a custom time in minutes or mm:ss format.
  • The timer will use the selected limit and waits for you to start.

Starting & Stopping

  • Tap Start to begin the timer; the button toggles to Stop while running.
  • Stopping the timer logs the end of the current period and advances the period counter (P1–P4).
  • When a countdown reaches zero it plays the whistle, logs the end of the period, and resets to the chosen time limit.

Audio & Mute

  • A whistle sound plays when the timer reaches zero.
  • Use the Mute button (lower-right) to toggle sound on/off; this setting is saved across reloads and resets.

Score Tracking

  • Tap a team's score area to increment that team's score. The big red/blue score tiles are tappable.
  • Each score entry is saved in the history with the team name, score snapshot (e.g. 2-1), and a timestamped elapsed label.
  • Tap the ✕ icon on a history row to remove that entry; removing a score will decrement the associated team's total.

Teams & Colors

  • Tap a team name while the timer is stopped to open the edit panel for that team (name and color).
  • The edit panel contains a name field and a color selector. As you change the color the small swatch updates immediately so you can preview the selection.
  • Saving updates the team name, the large score tile color, and all existing history rows for that team. Colors are persisted in local storage.
  • History rows display a small colored badge and show the team name in the chosen color for quick visual reference.

Period & Manual Adjustments

  • Tap the period display while stopped to cycle through P1–P4.
  • While the timer is running, tap the Elapsed display to open quick adjustment buttons (+/- 1 min, +/- 5 min) to correct early or late starts.

Reset & Saved State

  • Tap Reset to clear timer, scores, period, and history. Team names, selected colors, time limit, and mute state are preserved.
  • The app stores its state (timer, scores, period, history, team names/colors, time limit, mute) in local storage so reloading the page restores the previous session.

Notes & Tips

  • Use the swatches and history badges to quickly distinguish teams during live scoring.
  • Small-screen behavior: edit panels expand to full width and stack vertically for easier input on phones.
